Frequently Asked Questions

What types of small businesses do you serve?

We specialize in medical practices, dental offices, law firms, restaurants, and other small businesses with 1-50 employees. Our solutions are tailored to meet the specific compliance and operational needs of each industry.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

Emergency service calls guarantee a 2-hour response time for critical issues that impact your business operations. We offer 24/7 emergency support including weekends and holidays.

Do you handle HIPAA compliance for medical offices?

Yes! Our Professional Plus plan includes HIPAA compliance checks, secure patient data handling, and regular security audits specifically designed for medical and dental practices.

Can you support restaurant POS systems?

Absolutely. Our Restaurant Pro plan includes POS system monitoring, kitchen display support, backup internet solutions, and priority emergency support to keep your business running during peak hours.
